PRICING FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

What does the price include?

Every order includes all three ACCA manuals bundled together: Manual J (room-by-room heating & cooling load calculation), Manual D (duct design & sizing), and Manual S (equipment selection). Also included: inspection-ready documentation, code compliance verification, and quality review. No add-ons, no surprises.

Is 48-hour rush service an extra cost?

Yes. Rush service (48-hour turnaround) adds $0.04 per sq ft to your base rate. Rush availability is subject to current workload — contact us before ordering to confirm we can accommodate your timeline.

How do volume discounts work?

Volume discounts are based on your annual order total. At 11–20 orders per year you automatically receive 10% off every order. At 21+ orders per year, you receive 15% off. Volume discounts and Contractor+ membership (5%) do not stack — whichever is greater applies.

Can I get a custom quote for a large or complex project?

Yes. For unusually large projects, multi-building developments, or complex custom scenarios, contact us directly for a tailored quote. We'll provide a transparent price based on the scope of work.
