THE STORY

Where This Started

I spent years as a lead installer watching third-party load calculations blow up jobs. Wrong sizes, failed inspections, callbacks — it was costing time and money and nobody seemed to care as long as they got paid.

So I started doing them myself. Learned the methodology inside and out, built a process that works, and started getting reports that actually passed first time.

I grew up in this industry — both my parents were in it. This isn't a side hustle. It's the work I know best, and I take it seriously.
