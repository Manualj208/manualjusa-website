THE STORY

Where This Started

After years as a lead installer, I saw firsthand how inaccurate load calculations were creating problems for contractors — wrong equipment sizes, inspection delays, and costly callbacks. I knew there had to be a better way to approach this work.

I committed to learning the methodology thoroughly and building a careful, detail-oriented process. My goal with every report is to give contractors the accurate information they need to do their jobs well.

This industry is in my blood — I come from a family of tradespeople and have spent my career focused on getting load calculations right for contractors across the western US.
