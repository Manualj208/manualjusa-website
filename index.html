FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Manual J load calculation?

A Manual J load calculation is an ACCA-approved technical analysis that determines the exact heating and cooling capacity needed for a home or building. It accounts for climate zone, insulation, window area, occupancy, and dozens of other factors to ensure your HVAC equipment is properly sized — not oversized or undersized.

How long does a Manual J report take?

Standard turnaround is 5 business days. For urgent projects, we offer 48-hour rush service. Rush availability is subject to current workload — contact us to confirm availability before ordering.

What does Manual J USA charge for a load calculation?

Pricing starts at $250 flat for homes under 2,000 sq ft. Homes 2,000–4,000 sq ft are priced at $0.15/sq ft. Homes over 4,000 sq ft are $0.17/sq ft. All prices include the full bundle: Manual J, Manual D, and Manual S reports. See our full pricing page for volume discounts.

Are your reports accepted for building permits?

Yes. Our reports are prepared using ACCA-approved methodology and industry-standard software, formatted for inspection and permit submission in all six states we serve. We have a strong track record of permit acceptance across Idaho, Oregon, Washington, Arizona, New Mexico, and Utah.

Do I need Manual J, D, and S together?

Most jurisdictions requiring a Manual J also require Manual S (equipment selection) and often Manual D (duct design) for new construction and major HVAC replacements. We bundle all three in every order at no extra cost — one package, one price, everything you need for permit approval.

What states do you serve?

We serve Idaho, Oregon, Washington, Arizona, New Mexico, and Utah. Our team has specialized knowledge of each state's climate zones and local building code requirements. View our full service area details.
